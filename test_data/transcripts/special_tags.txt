Add tag is <add>for text insertions</add>

Column break <cb/> to indicate a break between columns

Date <date when="2020-12-09">December 9, 2020</date> to indicate a date, with an attribute of when

Figure tag <figure>drawing or graph here</figure> to indicate a figure or illustration in the text

Footnote <footnote marker="*">This is a footnote</footnote> contains the body of a footnote within the original document. This tag should be placed where the footnote marker appears in the text.

Gap <gap>page is torn</gap> for missing text

<head>Heading</head>
for headings (similar to using ==wiki heading mark-up==)

Stamp <stamp>stamp here</stamp> for stamps on envelops

<table>
  <row>
    <cell>Cell in a table</cell>
  </row>
</table>

<unclear>Unclear </unclear> for text that is difficult to read

Strike <strike>through</strike> or <s>s</s> or <hi rend="str">str</hi> for struck through text

Superscript <sup>sup</sup> or <hi rend="sup">hi rend=sup</hi> for superscript text

<u>'U</u> or <hi rend="underline">underline</hi> for underlined text

Expan <expan orig="original text">expanded text</expan> for expanding abbreviations, for example: <expan orig="qáµƒtorsimes">quatorsiemes</expan>

Reg <reg orig="original text">regularized text</reg> for regularizing text

Abbr <abbr expan="expanded text">abbreviated text</abbr>

<lb break="no"/>  a soft break tag.  In non-verbatim exports, combines the end of the line with the beginning of the next when a word is broken across the lines.

Marginalia <marginalia>marginalia here</marginalia>  for noting marginalia

Catchword <catchword>catchword</catchword> for catchwords
